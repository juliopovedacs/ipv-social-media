TECHNOLOGY AND IPV

Intimate Partner Violence (IPV) is a global societal issue that affects millions worldwide [1]:

Abusers are utilizing digital technologies to monitor and harass their victims [2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7].

OUR PROJECT

We are obtaining social media posts to understand how survivors of IPV seek and receive technology advice.

Our goal is to provide insights into how various stakeholders can improve the design of technology advice for IPV survivors and improve its sharing.
